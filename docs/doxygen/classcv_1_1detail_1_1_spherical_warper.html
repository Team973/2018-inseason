Detailed Description

Warper that maps an image onto the unit sphere located at the origin.

Projects image onto unit sphere with origin at (0, 0, 0) and radius scale, measured in pixels. A 360Â° panorama would therefore have a resulting width of 2 * scale * PI pixels. Poles are located at (0, -1, 0) and (0, 1, 0) points.
